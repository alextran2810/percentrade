1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Percentrade ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and mobile applications. By using our services, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status, to make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

Types of Cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • First-Party Cookies: Cookies set by Percentrade directly
  • Third-Party Cookies: Cookies set by our partners and service providers

5. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in securely across sessions
  • Security: To protect against fraudulent activity and enhance account security
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and customization choices
  • Analytics: To understand how you use our services and identify areas for improvement
  • Advertising: To deliver relevant ads and measure campaign performance
  • Performance: To optimize loading times and user experience

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

6.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Delete all cookies from your browser
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from specific websites
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Important: Blocking or deleting cookies may impact your ability to use certain features of our website and may require you to re-enter login credentials.

6.2 Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

6.3 Opt-Out Tools

You can also use industry opt-out tools:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) Opt-Out
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) Opt-Out
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on

6.4 Mobile Device Settings

For mobile devices, you can manage tracking preferences:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Allow Apps to Request to Track
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers support a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to DNT signals. We do not currently respond to DNT browser signals but respect your right to control your data through the methods described above.
